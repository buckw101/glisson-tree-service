## What This Is

The CRM (Customer Relationship Manager) is a private dashboard that connects directly to your website. Every time someone submits an estimate request or fills out a contact form on your site, a new lead automatically appears here — organized, tracked, and ready to act on.

No more missed leads buried in your email inbox. No more sticky notes or mental tracking of who called when. Everything is in one place.

---

## The Pipeline — How It Works

Your leads move through 6 stages from first contact to completed job:

```
New Lead → Contacted → Estimate Scheduled → Estimate Given → Job Booked → Completed
```

Each lead card moves through the board as you work it. You can drag cards from column to column, or click a lead and change its stage in one click.

**At a glance you can see:**
- How many leads are in each stage
- The total dollar value sitting in each stage
- Which leads need immediate attention
- Which jobs are booked and scheduled

---

## The Dashboard Stats

The four numbers at the top update automatically as you work leads:

| Stat | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Leads** | Every lead that came in this month |
| **Active Pipeline Value** | Combined estimated value of all open leads |
| **Booking Rate** | What percentage of leads you're closing into jobs |
| **Avg Booked Job** | Your average job value based on what you've closed |

---

## What Each Lead Card Shows

Every lead card displays:

- **Name** — who submitted the request
- **Service** — what they need (Tree Removal, Emergency, Stump Grinding, etc.)
- **Phone number** — click to call directly from the dashboard
- **Estimated job value** — so you know which leads to prioritize
- **Where they came from** — Website Form, Chat Widget, or Phone Call
- **Notes** — any details about the job

**Color-coded priority:**
- 🔴 Red dot = Urgent (emergency calls, active storm damage)
- 🟠 Orange dot = High priority (large job, ready to book)
- No dot = Normal

---

## Clicking a Lead — The Detail Panel

Click any card and a full detail panel slides in from the right. From here you can:

**Change the stage** — click any stage button to instantly move the lead through the pipeline

**Call them right now** — click "Call Now" to dial directly from your phone or computer

**Add notes** — log what was discussed, what was quoted, what they said, when to follow up

**One-click actions:**
- Mark as Contacted
- Schedule Estimate
- Mark as Booked
- Remove lead (if spam or duplicate)
