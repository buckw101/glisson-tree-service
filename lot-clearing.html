FAQ

Land Clearing Questions

Land clearing pricing is based on acreage, vegetation density, terrain difficulty, and debris disposal requirements. We always provide a free on-site estimate so you get an accurate price before any work begins. Call (478) 258-8169 to schedule your assessment.
Our standard clearing includes felling and removing all trees, removing brush and undergrowth, and hauling away all debris. Stump grinding can be added. We can leave the land at various stages of finish — from cleared but rough to fully graded and ready for construction.
Permit requirements depend on your location and project scale. Some counties require land disturbance permits for large-scale clearing. We can advise you on what's typically required in your county and you'll work with your local planning office to obtain necessary permits.
Timeline depends on the size and density of the project. A residential lot may take 1–2 days, while larger multi-acre commercial projects may take a week or more. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we provide your estimate.
