Tree on Your Home

A fallen tree on your roof, wall, or structure is a life-safety emergency. We respond as fast as humanly possible and work to protect your home from further damage.

Safety Tips

What to Do While You Wait for Us

1

Stay Away from the Tree

Keep everyone — family, pets, and neighbors — away from fallen or damaged trees. They can shift or fall further.

2

If Near Power Lines

Assume downed lines are live and dangerous. Stay at least 30 feet away and call your utility company in addition to us.

3

Document the Damage

Take photos and video from a safe distance before any cleanup. This helps enormously with insurance claims.

4

Call Your Insurance

If the tree damaged a structure, notify your homeowner's insurance. We can provide documentation they'll need for your claim.
